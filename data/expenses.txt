Maximum limits for expenses are:
- Flights: $600 per flight.
- Meals: $90 per meal, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
- Taxis and Rideshares: $75 per taxi or rideshare.
- Hotels: $200 for each night in a hotel.
- Other expenses: $50 per day.

To submit a claim, send an email to expenses@contoso.com with scanned receipts attached.
