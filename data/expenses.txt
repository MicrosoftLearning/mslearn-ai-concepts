Maximum limits for expenses:
- The limit for flights is $600 per flight.
- The limit for meals is $90 per meal, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
- The limit for taxis and Rideshares is $75 per taxi or rideshare.
- The limit for hotels is $200 for each night in a hotel.
- The limit for other expenses is $50 per day.

To submit a claim, send an email to expenses@contoso.com with scanned receipts attached.
